About company

Galaxion is a manufacturer of high-tech gaming equipment for casinos and arcades. We implement innovations in software, design and materials that allow us to successfully operate in international markets. The company's gaming equipment is supplied and works in Romania, the Czech Republic, Albania, Serbia, Spain, Kazakhstan, Moldova, Georgia, Mexico, Bolivia, Colombia, Peru.

We offer a full range of services: from production to logistics and service. Our representative offices are located in Spain, Georgia, Peru and Colombia.

Galaxion develops unique remote control systems for monitoring the game process in real time, providing reliable financial and technical control. All our products are certified and meet international standards.

Constant investments in development allow us to expand the range of services provided for gaming halls and casinos.

Online monitoring system

The system developed by the Galaxion company fully complies with the requirements of the legislation, including the Law of Ukraine "On State Regulation of Gambling Games" No. 768-IX dated 07/14/2020 (Articles 2, 9, 12).

System components:

  • Cashier, bartender and registration computers
  • Autobonusing modules
  • Multi-level jackpot, Happy hour JP and Crazy evening JP
  • Collection through the tablet without stopping the hall
  • Mobile application and self-service terminal
  • Website for monitoring gaming halls
  • SCS (Bet Control System) — software controlling the entire system, with gaming machine controllers (KIA/SMIB) for communication with machines via RS485
  • Central database

Our system was created for the international gaming business and has been used in casinos and gaming halls 24/7 for more than fifteen years. During this time, it proved its reliability, became an indispensable tool for keeping records and received many positive reviews.
